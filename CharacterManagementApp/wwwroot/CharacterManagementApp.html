  • General:
    Use the top menu buttons to navigate between pages.
  • Main:
    Use the left sub-menu buttons to access various game utilities.
  • New Character:
    Fill out the new character form and submit to add a character to the database.
  • View Character:
    Select a character to view from the list of existing characters. The 'Delete' button at the bottom of the page will remove the character from the database, if desired.
  • Update Character:
    Build a dynamic update form by selecting individual characters and the attributes you wish to update. Choose new values for the desired attributes on the form and submit to update the selected characters' information.
  • Inventory
    Add a new item to a character's inventory by selecting a character and submitting the item details form, or view the details of items currently in a character's inventory and update their quantity, if desired.
  • Spells:
    Add a new spell to a character's list of known spells by selecting a character and submitting the spell details form, or view the details of any spells known by characters already.
